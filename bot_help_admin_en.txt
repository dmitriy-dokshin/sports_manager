/new - Create a new game
/schedule <cron> - Set game schedule  (UTC). For example, "/schedule 30 18 * * 2" - https://crontab.guru
/delete_schedule - Remove game schedule
/list - Show last game players
/list_aloud - Show and mention last game players
/call_undecided - Mention undecided players among those who came before
/call_unpaid - Mention those who unpaid
/minus @ (can specify multiple) - mark persons who are not coming
